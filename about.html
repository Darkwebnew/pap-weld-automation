Our Story

Built on Engineering.
Trusted by Industry.

PAP Weld Core Automation Private Limited was founded in 1999 with a clear mission — to build reliable, high-performance industrial machines that Indian manufacturers could trust unconditionally.

Over 25 years, we have grown from a small fabrication workshop in Chennai into a full-scale industrial automation manufacturer serving clients across India — from heavy fabrication units in Tamil Nadu to structural steel companies in Vizag and automotive suppliers in Pune.

Every machine we build is engineered in-house, manufactured at our Vellanur facility, and commissioned on-site by our technical team. We do not resell. We manufacture.
