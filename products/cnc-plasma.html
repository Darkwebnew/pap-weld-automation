Technical Overview

CNC Plasma Cutting Machine

Our Industrial Series CNC Plasma Cutting Machines are engineered for high-speed, high-accuracy metal cutting in heavy fabrication environments. Every machine is designed in-house at our Chennai facility, assembled with industrial-grade servo motors and rack-and-pinion drive systems, and tested before dispatch. We offer standard widths of 1500mm, 2000mm, 3000mm, 4000mm, and 6000mm — and custom sizes on request.
